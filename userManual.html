    🚀 Getting Started

    Learn how to access and navigate Happy Place.

    System Requirements

    • A device with internet access (desktop, laptop, tablet)
    • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
    • Optional: A registered account to save your progress

    How to Access

    1. Open a web browser and go to [Website URL].

    2. If required, log in with your account credentials or create a new account.

    3. After logging in, you'll be directed to the home page where you can access all features.

    Home Page Navigation

    • The **menu** allows access to our different tools (Whiteboard, Journal, Checklist, Mood Tracker.)
    • The Go To Your Happy Place button takes you to the customizable workstation, where you can arrange tools according to your preference.

    ✨ Features Overview

    🎨 Whiteboard

    Sketch and save your drawings with ease.

    • Click on “Whiteboard” from our home page's menu.
    • Hold down the mouse and move across the canvas to start drawing!
    • Use the slider on the brush resizer to resize your pen.
    • To the left of the brush resizer is a color picker where you can select your color or manually type in the RGB.
    • Hit the Clear button to clear your canvas completely.
    • Hit the Eraser button to open the eraser pen.
    • Hit the Drawing Mode button to go back to the drawing pen.
    • Hit the Save button to save your drawing!

    📝 Journal

    Write and save journal entries.

    • Click on “Journal” from our home page’s menu.
    • Enter the title of your entry under "Entry Title"
    • Type your entry in the “Today’s Entry” Box.
    • Use the B (Bold), I (Italicize), and U (Underline) buttons to customize your entries.
    • Hit the Submit button to save your Journal Entry!

    📋 Self-Care Checklist

    • Click on "Checklist" from our main page's menu.
    • To add a new task, type it into the “Add a new task description” box, and hit the Add Task button.
    • Check off completed tasks by clicking the box to the left of said task.
    • Delete tasks by clicking the X to the left of said task.
    • Hit the Submit button to save your Checklist!

    😊 Mood Tracker

    • Click on the date picker next to "Select Date:" and input the date you want to log a mood for.
    • Click on one of the emojis that best represents your mood.
    • To add a custom mood, enter a custom mood description in the "Enter mood" text box, then enter a corresponding emoji in the "Enter emoji" text box.
    • Click the "Add Mood" button to save your mood log for the selected date.

    🌈 Color Changing Mood Tracker

    • Select a mood from the list that best represents how you're feeling.
    • Once a mood is selected, the background color of the page will automatically change.
    • Each mood has a unique color associated with it to provide a visual cue for your emotional state.
    • Click on different moods to see how the background color changes in response.

    🎵 Ambient Sounds

    • Choose from our three calming sounds (Rain, Forest, and Ocean)
    • Listen to the calm ambient sounds as you navigate the rest of our features!

    🖥️ Customizable Workstation

    • Add elements to your personal workstation.
    • Move, resize, or remove elements as needed.
    • Save your customized layout.

    Common problems and solutions.

    Problem: Features not loading properly.

    Solution: Refresh the page, check browser compatibility.
